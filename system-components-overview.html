System Components Overview

Veloblock operates through a series of smart contracts on Soroban. Each part of the system works together to provide seamless cross-border transactions and flash loan services:

  • Payment Rail Contracts: Handle sending and receiving funds across borders.
  • Liquidity Pools: Manage locked assets that back flash loans and support stable operations.
  • Collateral Module: Ensures deposited assets remain secure and fairly valued.
  • Oracle Feeds: Update contract parameters with real-time rates and asset prices.

Together, these components allow for trust-minimized global transfers and short-term loans, bridging real-world business needs with decentralized technology.
