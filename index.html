Overview of Veloblock

Veloblock is a DeFi protocol built on Soroban by Stellar. It focuses on helping businesses and individuals access cross-border payments and on-demand flash loans. Users lock supported assets as collateral, and upon repayment, accrued interest is distributed according to the smart contract logic.

Veloblockâ€™s cross-border payment network is especially useful for organizations that transact globally, eliminating barriers and reducing fees often found in traditional banking. By integrating with Soroban, Veloblock ensures transparent, secure, and scalable functionality for its community.

As a result, Veloblock empowers anyone to move value globally and secure short-term liquidity without lengthy approval processes. Our mission is to give businesses and individuals the freedom to transact and access credit in a more open and efficient way.
