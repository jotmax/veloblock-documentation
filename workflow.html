Workflow

This is a typical flow for using Veloblock:

  1. Lock collateral (e.g., XLM or other supported assets) in the Veloblock contract.
  2. Initiate a cross-border payment or request a flash loan against your collateral.
  3. Use the borrowed funds for business transactions or short-term liquidity needs.
  4. Repay the borrowed amount plus accrued interest within the agreed timeframe.
  5. Retrieve your collateral once the loan is settled.

Every operation executes through secure Soroban smart contracts, ensuring transactions are transparent, swift, and censorship-resistant.
